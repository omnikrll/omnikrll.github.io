Lately I've been doing this thing where I do smooth jazz DJ sets under the name Cream Soda and dance around with a bag on my head.

Passive Tones

Passive Tones (Karl Hohn & Ezra Teboul) are just two boys and their noise toys. They create lush, richly textured instrumental music from a palette of analog instruments, homemade effects, 8-bit electronics, and thrift-store cassettes. Their debut EP was released on Afternoons Modeling in December of 2016, and a second EP will be released soon.

What Does This Woman's Work Mean?

A mashup of Kate Bush's "This Woman's Work" with Justin Bieber's "What Do You Mean?" that Garret Harkawik and I made. Featured on The AV Club!

Cloud Circuits

From 2015-2017 I hosted a bi-weekly web radio show on Radio Bel-Air. Cloud Circuits featured a mix of ambient electronic music and experimental composition alongside chiptune, VGM, and demoscene / keygen music in an attempt to explore the interplay between "high-art" and "low-art" forms of electronic music.

J.O.N.A.H. 3000 OST (2015)

Soundtrack for a game created for the Ludum Dare 34 game jam in December of 2015.

J.O.N.A.H. 3000 is about a half shark/half robotic walking slaughterhouse who has been servicing supermarkets for decades by manufacturing the meat of the world...but now it has a BEEF to grind with humanity...and you, lowly human are deep inside the belly of the beast!
