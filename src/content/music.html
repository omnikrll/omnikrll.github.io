MUSIC & SOUND

Brave Mouse Cartographer (2019-2020)

Brave Mouse Cartographer is a trilogy of ambient games created by Common Opera for which I created the scores. In each game, the player watches a bird flying over a semi-obscured landscape and attempts to draw a map of the world on paper. Each score is comprised of a set of short audio loops pinned to different parts of the map which fade in and out as they come into view. The first score was created entirely using cassette tape loops; while the second and third scores were recorded digitally, the experience of recording to tape informed the compositional approach.

Brave Mouse Cartographer I was recently awarded the Human Machine Award at A MAZE. TOTAL DIGITAL 2020.

A Very Large Gesture (Original Motion Picture Soundtrack) (July 2020)

A Very Large Gesture is the original score from the short documentary of the same name, which examines the story of Ted Pelkey, a northern Vermont man who after unsuccessfully trying to get a building permit for a large garage in his front yard for nearly ten years decided to erected a large wooden middle finger in his front yard.

The score was written by Garret Harkawik and features me on guitar.

Passive Tones self-titled EP (2016)

Passive Tones (Karl Hohn & Ezra Teboul) are just two boys and their noise toys. They create lush, richly textured instrumental music from a palette of analog instruments, homemade effects, 8-bit electronics, and thrift-store cassettes. Their debut EP was released on Afternoons Modeling in December of 2016.

Cloud Circuits (2015-2017)

From 2015-2017 I hosted a bi-weekly web radio show on Radio Bel-Air. Cloud Circuits featured a mix of ambient electronic music and experimental composition alongside chiptune, VGM, and demoscene / keygen music in an attempt to explore the interplay between "high-art" and "low-art" forms of electronic music.
