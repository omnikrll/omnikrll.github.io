Cloud Circuits

Listen at bel-air.org

Cloud Circuits is a bi-weekly radio show, airing every other Thursday at 7pm. The show is a mix of chiptune and video game music with synth pop, ambient electronic, new age, kosmische, glitch, and experimental music, exploring the interplay across "high art" and "low art" in the world of electronic music.

What Does This Woman's Work Mean?

A mashup of Kate Bush's "This Woman's Work" with Justin Bieber's "What Do You Mean?" that Garret Harkawik I made. Featured on The AV Club!

passive tones

Relying principally on a Game Boy, a Commodore 64, and the General MIDI sound set, passive tones is an exploration of lush ambient textures created from a pallette of lo-fi and obsolete electronics. Sometimes solo, sometimes in collaboration with Ezra Teboul.

J.O.N.A.H. 3000 OST (2015)

Soundtrack for a game created for the Ludum Dare 34 game jam in December of 2015.

J.O.N.A.H. 3000 is about a half shark/half robotic walking slaughterhouse who has been servicing supermarkets for decades by manufacturing the meat of the world...but now it has a BEEF to grind with humanity...and you, lowly human are deep inside the belly of the beast!
