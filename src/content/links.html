LINKS

Here are some links to some of my favorite places and people, on the Internet and in Real Life! :D

Radio Bel-Air

Community web radio station based in Bushwick, Brooklyn. I do my radio show Cloud Circuits here every other Thursday night, as well as contributing to our working groups for web work, operations support, and events organizing. We have a very eclectic and diverse range of programming, and we broadcast live from the Silent Barn 5 nights a week from Sunday - Thursday.

Pulsewave NYC

Pulsewave is the US’s longest running event dedicated to exploring the sights and sounds of Chip Music’s greatest musicians and motion visualists. Using vintage video game systems and computers as the basis and influence for stunning next generation music and visuals, Pulsewave spotlights emerging and established artists from the international and local community. Pulsewave promises to remain committed to showcasing the best the global Chip Music community has to offer.

I run sound for Pulsewave's monthly events, as well as running a live audio stream on Bel-Air.

Babycastles

Babycastles is another really cool space for the DIY community with a focus on video games, digital art, and music. They have lots of neat events like concerts and game jams. I volunteer here- I've helped install gallery shows, run sound, and work the door for events.

Silent Barn

A very cool DIY space for artists and musicians, and the home of Radio Bel Air. Silent Barn houses a venue, artists' studios, a boutique synth shop, a recording studio, a theater company, and more. It's a landmark of Brooklyn's DIY arts scene and one of my favorite places in the city.

p5.js

p5.js is a JavaScript library that starts with the original goal of Processing, to make coding accessible for artists, designers, and beginners, and reinterprets this for today's web.

I've made some contributions to the development of p5.sound, and I am currently contributing to the development of the new p5.js web editor.

MAGNET

MAGNET (Media and Games Network) is a collaborative space designed to foster cross pollination between the various disciplines of arts and sciences at NYU. I studied here in the Integrated Digital Media program at NYU Polytechnic School of Engineering, and I come back for things like game jams and conferences.

Garret Harkawik

Garret is a super talented filmmaker and musician, and he and I have worked together on many different projects over the years. Also a pretty swell guy!

Ezra Teboul

Ezra is my bandmate in Passive Tones, and a really brilliant guy. His work with hardware and his research and writing into the history of electronic music is really interesting!
