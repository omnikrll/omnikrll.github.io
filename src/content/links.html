LINKS

Here are some links to some of my favorite places and people, on the Internet and in Real Life! :D

Babycastles

Babycastles is a really cool space for the DIY community in video games, art, and music. They have lots of neat events like concerts and game jams. Sometimes I volunteer here.

Radio Bel Air

Community web radio station based in Bushwick, Brooklyn. I do my radio show Cloud Circuits on Bel Air, as well as contributing as a member of the Events and Web working groups. We have a very eclectic and diverse range of programming, broadcasting 5 nights a week from Sunday - Thursday.

Silent Barn

Another very cool DIY space for artists and musicians, and the home of Radio Bel Air. Silent Barn houses a venue, artists' studios, a recording studio, a theater company, and more. It's a landmark of Brooklyn's DIY arts scene and one of my favorite places in the city.

MAGNET

MAGNET (Media and Games Network) is a collaborative space designed to foster cross pollination between the various disciplines of arts and sciences at NYU. I studied here in the Integrated Digital Media program at NYU Polytechnic School of Engineering, and I come back for things like game jams and conferences.

Garret Harkawik

Garret is a super talented filmmaker and musician, and he and I have worked together on many different projects over the years. Also a pretty swell guy!
