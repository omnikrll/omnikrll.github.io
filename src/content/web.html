nullaby (breathe easy)

nullaby (breathe easy) is a virtual interactive meditation chamber, employing sound, music, and imagery to promote relaxation and mental healing. The intent is to provide an island of tranquility in the increasingly turbulent and chaotic world of cyberspace, as well as exploring the nature of spirituality in the digital age by automating the process of guided meditation.

Built with p5.js for NewHive's "Relaxation Machine" open call. Source code here.

Going Dark

Going Dark is a malevolent AI simulator, created for the Ludum Dare 33 "You Are The Monster" game jam in August of 2015. You control the AI of The Wraith, a cruiser on a mission into deep space. Your crew have come out of cryo stasis too early and now pose a threat to the successful completion of your mission: in their inept attempts to regain control of the ship they are inadvertently corrupting your core memory. Thwart their efforts by correctly entering a random string of text in the text box. Succeed and a bit more their life support is cut off; fail and another part of your memory is corrupted and lost, until you are forced into a cold reboot.

Built with jQuery. Art and writing by Sam DiBella. Source code here.

Resonant Filter Bank

This browser instrument routes the audio of an HTML5 media element through a bank of 13 fixed frequency resonant filters. The architecture of the filter bank is roughly based on that of the Moog 914 Fixed Filter Bank. Best viewed in Chrome.

Built with pure JavaScript and HTML5 at the July 2014 Monthly Music Hackathon. Source code here.
